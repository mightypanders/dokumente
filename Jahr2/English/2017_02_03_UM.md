# Meetings

There will be listening comprehension. Just not today.

## What is a 
### progress meeting
How far along is a given project?  
State of the project, what may be roadblocks?
### brainstorming meeting
Gather new ideas for a project  
Gather solutions for a problem
### shareholder meeting
a meeting concering everybody with a share of the company  
everybody whoe owns a part of the company  
### kickoff meeting
the begin of a new project, a roadmap is laid out  
motivational actions are taken  
### team meeting
an internal meeting, may be used to discuss problems  
topics are related to the team not a specific project
### board meeting
the management of an organization, CEOs, Union Leaders etc.
